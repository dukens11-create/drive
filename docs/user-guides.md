# User Guides

## Passenger / customer guide
- Create account and complete profile setup.
- Book immediate or scheduled rides and track trip progress.
- Discover restaurants, place food orders, and track delivery status.
- Add payment methods, manage wallet/promos, and review receipts.
- Submit ratings/reviews and open support tickets when needed.

## Driver guide
- Complete driver registration and document verification.
- Manage availability and accept/complete rides and delivery jobs.
- Use navigation/location sharing and in-trip safety features.
- Review earnings, payouts, and historical trip activity.
- Contact support for disputes, incidents, or account help.

## Restaurant guide
- Register restaurant and complete compliance verification.
- Manage menus, pricing, categories, and item availability.
- Process incoming orders and coordinate with delivery partners.
- Monitor operational analytics and fulfillment performance.
- Use support channels for onboarding and dispute resolution.

## Admin guide
- Navigate dashboard for platform health and live operations.
- Manage users, drivers, restaurants, rides, and orders.
- Handle payments, refunds, promos, risk signals, and audits.
- Review reports/KPIs and tune platform settings.
- Follow troubleshooting and escalation procedures for incidents.

## Support agent guide
- Triage incoming tickets with category and severity labels.
- Resolve common disputes (billing, cancellation, fulfillment, account).
- Use clear and empathetic communication with customers/partners.
- Escalate security, fraud, and critical safety cases to on-call owners.
- Close loop by documenting resolution and follow-up actions.
